Toastr

A "Toastr" is a notification that pops up at a specified position. They are available in the same 4 styles as alerts, and can be set to either self-dismiss after a specified amount of time or stay open until manually dismissed. A Toastr is always dismissable by clicking on it. They can be set to appear top right (default, preferred), any of the other 3 corners, or centered. Whichever position is used should remain consistent throughout the application.
