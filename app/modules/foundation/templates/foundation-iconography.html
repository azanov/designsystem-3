Corporate Logo

Our logo is the cornerstone of our visual identity. It captures the essence of what makes us unique, and symbolizes how we create meaningful impact for our clients, and their customers, in the world of commerce. ​

View approved logos on Brand Central. When applying the logo, be sure to reference all of our logo brand guidelines (PDF), which you can also find on Brand Central.​

Icon Strategy

Our family of icons and illustrations provides a core set that’s also extensible, which enables you to apply our iconography to deeper areas of your product as needed.

In general, we use illustrative icons in our product marketing — these reflect our brand’s broad concepts. For product functionality we use functional icons — these reflect actions, tasks, settings and other practical aspects of our products.

Download the complete Brand Guidelines (PDF).

Functional Icons

Our functional icons are designed to convey a product’s commands, categories or common actions visually. Unlike our illustrations, they are “live’ objects in that they are used for navigation or button commands.

These icons are provided as fonts along with the Design System and are used within HTML with an "i" tag using classes of the font name and the icon name, as follows:

The Design System package includes 2 commercial icon fonts, Nucleo Icon Mini and Nucleo Icon Outline. Nucleo Mini is to be used for on-screen icons under 24 pixels, and Nucleo Outline is to be used for 24 pixels and larger.

We also provide a custom Design System font called "PBfont". This font is periodically updated with custom icons as needed by PB applications. The class name for these icons (shown below) is pbfont
