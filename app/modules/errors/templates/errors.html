Guidelines

Error pages should:
  • Spell out the problem in plain, friendly, non-alarmist language
  • Never contain exclamation points or question marks in headers
  • Provide the user with ideas for solving the problem
  • Provide only highly relevant links
  • Never exceed six columns in our grid
  • Include a support contact
  • Format headers in sentence caps with a concluding period
  • Format bullet copy in sentence caps with no concluding period
