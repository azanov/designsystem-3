Hamburger Menus

  • Push
  • Slide

Hamburger menus are good options for space-challenged designs. Like split views, they offer a persistent set of sub-level menu options. But they add responsive side navigation and dynamically collapsible/expandible primary navigation.

Jasny Bootstrap v3.1.3

Push

The “push” style expands the side navigation and pushes all other page content to the right. Page content is dimmed and the side navigation can be collapsed again via a “close” button.

Slide

The “slide” style leaves the page content in place and overlays the side navigation. The side navigation can be collapsed again by clicking on the hamburger menu icon.
