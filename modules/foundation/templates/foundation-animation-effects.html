Effects

Classic motion and animation effects include:

  • Fade In/Fade Out with a change in opacity.
  • Move by changing X- and Y-axis positioning.
  • Slide by changing X- and Y-axis positioning and opacity.
  • Grow/Shrink by increasing or decreasing scale.
  • Color Transition by changing color values.
  • Flip by rotating along the Y-axis.
  • Spin with full 360-degree rotation.
  • Fall/Rise by changing scale and opacity, and increasing Z-axis elevation.
  • Pop by changing scale and increasing Z-axis elevation.
  • Punch by changing scale and decreasing Z-axis elevation.
  • Shake by rapidly moving back and forth horizontally along the X-axis.
  • Jiggle by moving back and forth vertically along Y-axis.
